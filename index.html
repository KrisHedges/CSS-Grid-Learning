Grid

  • display grid (or inline-grid)
  • grid-template-columns 20% auto 20% (ordered list of column widths)
  • grid-template-rows 60% 40% (ordered list of column rows)

Columns

  • grid-column (column number) / span (number)

Rows

  • grid-row (row number)
